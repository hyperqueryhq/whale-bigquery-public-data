* [STRING]    `CancerType`
  - Type(s) of cancer analyzed
* [STRING]    `Location`
  - Body location that was analyzed
* [INTEGER]   `Subjects`

* [STRING]    `Collections`
  - Original data collections analyzed
* [STRING]    `AnalysisArtifactsonTCIA`
  - Types of analysis artifacts produced
* [DATE]      `Updated`
  - Most recent update reported by TCIA
